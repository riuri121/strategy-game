## Current Status

**Locked In:**
- Core concept: Single-player economic sandbox, Victoria 3-inspired
- Earth factions: USA, Europe, China, India (demand sinks, event-driven)
- Space factions: AI corporations competing with player (MVP: identical)
- Production system: Facilities → recipes → operation styles
- Location economics: Distance-based import/export penalties
- Demand categories: War, consumption, development, research
